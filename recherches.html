My Researches

Scientific exploration and contribution to the advancement of knowledge

Research domain

Machine learning models learns from data to be able to acomplish a given task. However, even if the representations learned form classical methods perform well, these repesentations can hardly perform on similar but different datasets.

To solve this problem, the research literature released Domain Adaptation algorithms, which are made to learn a robust and Domain invariant representations.

My studies are between Domain Adaptation and Generation. I develop algorithms that learns robust representations and mix Generation algorithm to generate realistic time series. My thesis is in colaboration between, Paul Wurth, a luxembourgish company specialized in the iron and steel industry, and The German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI), specialized in innovative AI-driven software technologies.
